Investing in your mental health and well being shouldn't ever feel like a waste of time.

Learn more about the different resources compiled here onto one site to help make it easier for CMU students to explore how they can prioritize their mental health.

Why mental health?

Not only is it important to take care of yourself physically, but it's also important to take care of your mental health and well being. Poor mental health can negatively affect all aspects of life and have long lasting impacts.

Click here to learn more about the impacts





Types of resources

There are a variety of resources so be sure to explore a few and find the one that works for you. On campus, there are organizations like Lean On Me and TimelyCare. CaPS is also available, but other resources like BetterHelp and eTherapyPro also provide online therapy and services that may be more accessible.

Click here for CMU resources and here for additional resources





Self-care practices

Everyone's journey with mental health is different. We encourage you to try out different practices until you find the strategies and tips that work well for you. Some common and popular self-care practices include meditation, journaling, therapy, exercise, reading, reflection, and time away from social media. What works best for you?
